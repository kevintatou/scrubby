# Competitors

Scrubby focuses on a tight, local-first clipboard workflow. Here is how it compares.

## biip (Rust)
- Focus: General-purpose log/secret sanitization.
- Workflow: File/log oriented, not clipboard-first.
- UX: More developer-centric, less “one hotkey” feel.
- Scrubby: Clipboard-first, hotkey-friendly, local/offline.

## Hanzo Guard
- Focus: Enterprise security and monitoring.
- Workflow: Policy-driven, multi-user and cloud-managed.
- UX: Heavy setup, centralized management.
- Scrubby: No setup, no admin, local/offline.

## LogLens sanitize
- Focus: Log pipeline cleaning.
- Workflow: Integrations for logging stacks and CI.
- UX: Optimized for DevOps pipelines.
- Scrubby: Personal workflow at the clipboard level.

## Browser extensions
- Focus: In-browser redaction or form scrubbing.
- Workflow: Browser-only, not system clipboard.
- UX: Limited to the browser context.
- Scrubby: Works everywhere, regardless of app.
